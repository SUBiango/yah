About Young Access Hub

Mission. Vision. Values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to hand young people the tools, the skills, and the fire to change their own lives and the communities around them.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every young person has equitable access to opportunities for growth regardless of their background.

Our Values

  • Empowerment
  • Innovation
  • Collaboration

Our Story

How It Started

Young Access Hub was born from the recognition that many young people face barriers in accessing the resources, skills, and networks they need to thrive. Founded in 2020, we began with a simple but powerful belief: every young person deserves the opportunity to transform their life and contribute meaningfully to their community.

Where We Are Today

Today, YAH is working on the 3rd youth empowerment summit in Kono District. We have planned and executed a number training programs for young people in the district. We were also able to lunch and ICT training center to equipe young people with essential digital skills. Due to human and other resource constraints, we have decided to desing, build and lunch a mobile e-learning app for young people to access in-demand skills from the comfort of their homes. .
