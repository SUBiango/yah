About the App

Learning Without Limits

Kantan is designed for young people in Sierra Leone who face barriers to quality education — whether due to geography, cost, or limited local resources. The app brings world-class learning directly to their mobile devices.

With an offline-first design, Kantan ensures learners can access content even with limited internet connectivity — a key challenge in rural areas like Kono District. Courses are developed in partnership with local educators and focus on practical, employable skills.

Features

Built for Young Sierra Leoneans

Every feature is designed around the real needs and constraints of our learners.

Video Lessons

Engaging, bite-sized video content taught by local and international experts, designed for mobile viewing.

Skill Assessments

Quizzes and practical assessments that test comprehension and reinforce learning after each module.

Progress Tracking

A personal dashboard showing completed courses, scores, streaks, and growth over time.

Peer Learning Groups

Cohort-based learning communities where students collaborate, discuss, and motivate each other.

Digital Certificates

Verifiable certificates upon course completion that learners can share with employers and institutions.

Mobile & Offline

Fully functional without a constant internet connection — download lessons and learn on the go.
