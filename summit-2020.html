Where It All Began

The Summit That Started a Movement

In 2020, Young Access Hub dared to dream big. What began as a vision became the first-ever YAH Youth Summit — bringing together over 150 young people from across Kono District for a historic day of inspiration and empowerment.

Under the theme "Rise Up, Step Forward," participants heard from local leaders and change-makers, attended hands-on workshops, and left with a sense of community and possibility that many described as life-changing. This inaugural edition laid the foundation for everything that followed.
