About This Summit

A Day That Moved a Generation

The 2022 YAH Youth Summit was a landmark moment — bigger, bolder, and more impactful than ever before. Over 100 young leaders from across Kono District gathered at Fachima Complex Hall for a full day of learning, connection, and inspiration.

Under the theme "Building Bridges, Breaking Barriers," participants engaged with world-class speakers, attended practical workshops on entrepreneurship, digital skills, and leadership — and left with a renewed sense of purpose and possibility.
