Our Speakers

Meet the local leaders, changemakers, and visionaries who are shaping our community.

His Worship Mayor Komba Sam

Mayor - Koidu New Sembehun City Council

Historic two-term mayor serving 169,000+ residents since 2018. Holds advanced degrees in Business Administration and Economics, championing youth empowerment and community transformation.

Osman Yaah

Innovator and Entrepreneur

Co-founder of Auto-Smart Irrigation & Energy Company Ltd and award-winning innovator. Winner of President Bio's 2024 Innovation Award ($30,000), transforming agriculture through climate-smart technology and empowering vulnerable communities.

Rebecca Yei Sam

Honourable Member of Parliament

First woman elected to represent Kono District in Parliament (2018, 2023). Chairperson for the Labour Committee in Parliament, championing women's empowerment and youth participation in decision-making.

Tamba Songu Mbriwa

Entrepreneur | Gemmologist | Business Consultant

CEO of Legacy Group of Companies with global business reach from Asia to Europe. Master's in Gemology, passionate mentor for young entrepreneurs, believing true leadership is measured by empowering others.
